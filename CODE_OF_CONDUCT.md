# Code Of Conduct

SectorForge is a community project for building practical sim-racing telemetry tools. We want issues, pull requests, and discussions to be useful, welcoming, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and communication styles.
- Keep feedback specific, constructive, and tied to the project.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating tone.
- Respect privacy. Do not post secrets, private game paths, telemetry captures, or personal information.
- Follow the repository's Windows-first and local-first project constraints when proposing changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory language or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Posting exploit details, secrets, or unsafe reproduction steps in public channels.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance or project scope after clarification.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers through the most private channel available for the repository. Include relevant links or context, but avoid sharing unnecessary personal information.
